The Last Resistance

I made the Last resistance in 48H game jam.
It's a first person survival game where you need to gather supplies like weapons and ammo to battle machine enemies.

The game is stylized with PS1 era style graphics, with low resolution camera, low res textures and low poly models.

In addition to the programming and design I also did all the textures and 3d models.

I also animated the enemy models and the player's weapon animations.

Key features
  • First person character controller
  • FPS weapon system (swapping and attacking)
  • Breakable barries and collectable items
  • Patrolling enemy AI
  • Cool robot enemies
