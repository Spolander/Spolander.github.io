Mnemonic

Mnemonic is a third person puzzle game with some stealth elements.
The gameplay loop consists of collecting items and listening tips that can be found as audio recordings.

The game has two levels, the 1st level is re-used again with minor changes to the level and it has a time limit.

On a higher difficulty the game also includes AI controlled security guards that patrol the area and try to escort the player away from the area.

The AI was done to the game as an afterthought and I wrote my bachelor's thesis about it.

Key features
  • Third person controller with root motion movement
  • Collecting clues and observing
  • Solving puzzles
  • Avoiding AI (on higher difficulty)
